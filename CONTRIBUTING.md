# Contributing to Bug Reports

Thanks for helping make PlumAntics mods better!

## For Players: How to Report

See [README.md](README.md) for complete instructions. TL;DR: Use the right form for your issue type, be specific about what happened, and search first for duplicates.

## For Other Creators: Reporting Compatibility Issues

If you're another mod creator and found a compatibility issue with PlumAntics mods, you can open an issue here. Use the [Compatibility Report form](../../issues/new?template=compatibility_report.yml).

## Code of Conduct

- Be respectful and kind
- No spam, ads, or off-topic content
- No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Focus on the issue, not the person
- Assume good intent from other players

We reserve the right to close or remove comments that violate this.
